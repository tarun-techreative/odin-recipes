Muffins

Description

According to allrecipes, this is one of those food items that taste best after letting it sit for at least a half-hour after initially making it.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango, peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 fresh jalapeno chile pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place mango, green onion, red bell pepper, cilantro, jalapeno, lemon juice, and lime juice in a medium bowl.
  3. Mix ingredients well. Cover and let sit for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  4. Serve with chips (or anything else you prefer).
