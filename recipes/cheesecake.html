Cheesecake

Description

With only 6 key ingredients, this cheesecake is a fairly simple item to make.

This should only take about 10 minutes to prepare, and 40 minutes to bake. All in all, the cheesecake should be ready to eat in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups dripped yogurt
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 2 teaspons vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon frontier coop lemon flavoring

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients until they are smooth.
  2. Pour into baking dish that is lightly greased with cooking oil.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees until the top is lightly brown and there are no particles sticking to the prongs of a fork when poking the cake with one. This can take at least 45 minutes, sometimes longer.
  4. Remove from oven and let it cool before placing in the fridge. Do not cut until the cheesecake has been cooled in the fridge.
