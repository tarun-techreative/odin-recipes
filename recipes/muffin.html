Muffins

Description

With at least 15 key ingredients, and 6 more optional ones, these banana muffins may not seem easy to make, but they are!

These should take about 20 minutes to prepare, and only 20 minutes to bake. All in all, they should be ready to eat in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 3 ripe bananas, smashed
  • 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained and squeezed dry
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup whole milks
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped toasted pecans

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. For batter, whisk together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and allspice in a small bowl.
  3. Whisk together bananas, egg, pineapple, white sugar, brown sugar, milk, vanilla, and melted butter in a large bowl.
  4. Fold in flour mixture until just combined. Fold in pecans.
  5. Add batter to the prepared muffin cups.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of muffins comes out clean, about 20â€“25 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes.
  7. Remove muffins and let cool completely.
